a game about the absurdity of love

🎮 play it here
https://amina-io.github.io/desire-game/

what is this???
you try to make two emotionally unavailable pixel people fall into each other's arms before the clock runs out. you control one character. 

how to play?!?

pick skin tone, gender, and their damage for both characters
choose a difficulty
use A/D or arrow keys to move your character
get close to the CPU and stay there to build connection time
drift apart and both characters lose HP. let it hit zero and it's over

mobile works too, on-screen buttons.
